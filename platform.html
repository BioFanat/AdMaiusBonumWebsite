Ad Maius Bonum Party Platform


Increasing Involvement in Renewable Energy Research - International

In an attempt to lessen carbon dioxide emissions, we will combine our efforts with those of other nations to research and develop more efficient renewable energy production methods.

Corporate Pollution Regulation - National

In an attempt to limit the amount of pollution and carbon emissions made by the US, we will implement stricter corporate restrictions. Corporations will have a limit of carbon emissions every year and will not be allowed to empty liquid or solid waste into bodies of water or surrounding land.

Purchasing Land for Protection - State

We will purchase untouched land from landowners and make it so that nothing can be built on the land, thus protecting it from mass pollution.

Increasing Number of Solar Power Plants - State

Is a method of utilizing clean energy, we will increase the number of solar power plants built by the state.

In-Depth Garbage Sorting - Local

The waste collection and sorting system in place is largely ineffective. There is no compost and many things cannot be recycled. We will add a compost system and improve the recycling system to leave as little waste as possible.

Requiring Environmental Science as a Course - School

By requiring that all students attend an environmental course to graduate highschool, we are educating students on environmental issues and methods to prevent the deterioration of the earthâ€™s climate and resources.

Specialized Food Waste Management - School

Food waste is a staggering problem in schools, because of the wasteful nature of students and the lunch systems. We propose a separate, specialized food waste system with bins to allow for food waste to be properly disposed of, benefiting the environment.
